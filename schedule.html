Programme

Main Conference: 24th-26th March

The main conference venue will be in the Conversation Quarter at the State Library of Victoria, right in the heart of Melbourne's downtown.

Tutorials, Doctoral Colloquium and Workshops: 27th-28th March

These events will take place in Storey Hall, 7th Floor, RMIT.
